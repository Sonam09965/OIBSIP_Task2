Hello Viewer!!


It’s pleasure to introduce myself to you.


My name is Sonam Ojha. I’m a deterministic and optimistic computer science student pursuing my Bachelor’s degree in the field of Computer Science from Chouksey Engineering College, Bilaspur (C.G). I’m basically from Buxar, Bihar but currently staying in Raigarh (C.G). I have done my schooling from St. Michael’s English Medium School, Raigarh (C.G).


I have keen interest in Web Development. I have good knowledge in the Frontend Development ,Web Designing, Responsive Web Designing, Graphic Designing and Problem Solving. I have good knowledge of C, C++, JAVA, PYTHON, HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, MYSQL, GIT, GITHUB, NODE.JS, REACT.JS


My "strengths" are – I’m self-motivated, an early adopter and also have great observation.My "Weaknesses" are – Easily trusting everyone and underestimating myself.


My hobbies are "LISTENING TO MUSIC" - which makes me feel relaxed, "DRAWING" - by which I express my inner thoughts and creativity, "GARDENING & PLANTING"gives me peace and relief from stress.
